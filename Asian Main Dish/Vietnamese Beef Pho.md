# Vietnamese Beef Pho

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] 3 cups of [Ox Tail Broth][1]
- [ ] Beef meat balls
- [ ] Beef slices
- [ ] Beef tripe
- [ ] 1 large white onion
- [ ] 2 stalks of green onion
- [ ] Beansprouts
- [ ] 1 lime
- [ ] 100g Rose branded Vietnamese vermicelli
- [ ] Thai basil
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Thai Top Choice brand satay sauce *(Optional)*
- [ ] Sriracha sauce *(Optional)*
- [ ] Hoisin sauce *(Optional)*

## 2. Broth & Noodles
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **3 cups** of beef broth | Beef meat balls |
| **1** white onion, sliced | Slices of beef briskets or ribeye |
| Beef tripe | **2** stalks of green onion |

1. Heat up the beef broth in a large pot with **2 cups** of hot water and beef meatballs.
2. Add the sliced onions into the broth and simmer for **10 minutes**.
3. Soak sliced beef in cold water for about **10 minutes**, changing the water once.
4. Add green onions, tripe, and sliced beef to the broth at the end, just before serving.

## 3. Noodles
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **100g** of Vietnamese vermicelli | **1/2 tsp** of salt |
| **1 handful** of beansprouts | Thai basil |
| **1** lime, cut into wedges | **2 tsp** of satay sauce *(Optional)* |
| Sriracha sauce | Hoisin sauce |

1. Soak the noodles in a pot of warm water (30-50°C) with a dash of salt for 10min
2. Strain noodles and add to the simmering broth
3. Rinse beansprouts and basil, and add into the noodles.
4. Remove from heat immediately to serve.
5. *Optional:* Add **2 tsp** of satay sauce.
6. Serve with lime wedges, sriracha sauce, and hoisin sauce on the side.
