# Ox Tail Broth

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] 2 beef tendons
- [ ] Shaoxing cooking wine
- [ ] 1 stalk of green onion
- [ ] 4 slices of ginger
- [ ] Table salt
- [ ] Chicken bouillon
- [ ] [Vietnamese Pho Spice Pouch][1] *(Optional)*

## 2. Pressure Cook Tendons
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **2** beef tendons | **12 segments** of ox tail |
| **1 stalk** of green onion, chopped roughly | **4 slices** of ginger |
| **2 tbsp** of Shaoxing cooking wine | **2 tsp** of salt |
| **2 tsp** of chicken bouillon | [Vietnamese Pho Spice Pouch][1] *(Optional)* |

1. Add beef tendons to a Instant Pot.
2. Fill the inner pot with water to cover the tendons completely.
3. Set the pressure cooker to steam for 5 minutes.
4. Release pressure and pour out the liquid. Rinse the tendons in cold water and return to the inner pot.
5. Add the cooking wine, green onions, and ginger to the pot.
6. Fill the inner pot with water until tendons are completely covered.
7. Set pressure cooker to **120 minutes** at high pressure.
8. When timer runs out, allow natural release.
9. Remove the ginger and green onion stalks
10. Adjust the broth with salt and chicken bouillon to taste.
11. *(Optional):* Bring the broth to a simmer in a new pot on medium-low heat.
12. *(Optional):* Add the spice pouch and allow it to simmer for the  30 minutes.
