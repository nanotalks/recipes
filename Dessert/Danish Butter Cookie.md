# Danish Butter Cookie

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] Large egg
- [ ] *(optional)* Lemon  
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] Imitation vanilla extract
- [ ] Granulated sugar
- [ ] Table salt
- [ ] All-purpose flour

## 2. Baking
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **250g** of butter, room temperature | **85g** of sugar |
| **1** egg | **1 tsp** of vanilla extract |
| **Pinch** of table salt | **284g** of flour |
| **1/4** a lemon zest *(optional)* | |

1. Mix the soften butter with the sugar on medium speed until combined and fluffy, 1 - 2 min
2. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the butter, and mix on medium speed until combined, 1 min
3. Add the salt, flour and optional lemon zest to the batter, and mix on low speed until combined
4. Pipe out the cookie, or roll out the dough about 1 cm thick, and press into shape
7. Place in fridge for 30min
8. Preheat the oven to 350°F
9. Bake for 12 - 15 min, until the cookie's edges turn lightly brown
10. Allow the cookies to rest before serving
