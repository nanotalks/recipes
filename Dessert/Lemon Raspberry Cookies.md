# Lemon Raspberry Cookies

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] **½ cup** butter
- [ ] **1** large egg
- [ ] **1.5 cups** fresh raspberries
- [ ] 1 lemon zest
- [ ] **1.5 cups** all-purpose flour
- [ ] Artificial vanilla extract
- [ ] **½ cup** granulated sugar
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Baking powder
- [ ] Baking soda

## 2. Mixing and Preparation
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **½ cup** butter, softened | **½ cup** granulated sugar |
| **1** large egg | **½ tsp** artificial vanilla extract |
| **¼ tsp** salt | **1** lemon, zested and juiced |
| **¼ tsp** baking powder | **¼ tsp** baking soda |
| **1.5 cups** all-purpose flour | **1.5 cups** fresh raspberries |

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Mix butter and sugar together in a stand mixer set on medium, until light and fluffy, approximately 3 minutes.
3. Reduce mixer to low speed and add the vanilla extract, egg, salt, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Mix well, scraping down the sides of the bowl when needed.
4. In a separate mixing bowl, sift together the baking powder, baking soda, and flour.
5. With the mixer on low, slowly add the dry ingredients into the dough.
6. Very carefully fold the raspberries into the dough manually, with a spactula.
7. Drop **1 heaping tablespoon** of the dough per cookie onto baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
8. Bake for approximately 15 minutes, until the cookie is just starting to turn golden brown around the edges.
9. Allow the cookies to cool for 10 minutes before serving
