# Spinach Crackers

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] 1/3 cup frozen loose leaf spinach
- [ ] 1 cup flour
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
- [ ] Table salt
- [ ] Baking powder
- [ ] Onion powder
- [ ] Garlic powder

## 2. Preparation
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **1/3 cup** frozen loose leaf spinach | **1** egg |
| **1 cup** flour | **1 tbsp** toasted sesame seeds |
| **1/2 tsp** table salt | **1/2 tsp** baking powder |
| **1/2 tsp** of onion powder | **1/2 tsp** of garlic powder |

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
2. Puree frozen loose leaf spinach in a food processor or blender.
3. Combine the spinach with the egg, flour, sesame seeds, table salt, baking powder, onion powder, garlic powder and **1 tbsp** water in a mixing bowl until combined.
4. Knead the dough into a ball.
5. Roll the dough out flat on parchment paper, about 1/4cm thick.
6. Use a knife or pizza cutter to cut the dough into squares.
7. Prick each cracker 2-3 times with a fork.
8. Bake for approximately 20 minutes until very lightly browned. Crackers around the edge may crisp up faster, so pull them out first if needed.
9. Cool for 5 min before breaking up the crackers
