# Spinach Crackers

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] 50g of frozen loose leaf spinach
- [ ] 1 cup flour
- [ ] Table salt
- [ ] Baking powder
- [ ] Onion powder
- [ ] Garlic powder
- [ ] 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

## 2. Preparation
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **50g** of frozen loose leaf spinach | **1** egg |
| **1 cup** of flour | **1 tbsp** of toasted sesame seeds |
| **1/2 tsp** of table salt | **1/2 tsp** of baking powder |
| **1/2 tsp** of onion powder | **1/2 tsp** of garlic powder |
| **1 tbsp** of butter, melted | |

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Puree frozen loose leaf spinach in a food processor or blender.
3. Combine the spinach with the egg, flour, sesame seeds, table salt, baking powder, onion powder, garlic powder and **1 tbsp** water in a mixing bowl until combined.
4. Spread the dough out flat on parchment paper, about 1/2cm thick. The dough will be very sticky, coat the surface of a roller or serving spoon with butter before spreading.
5. Use a knife or pizza cutter to cut the dough into squares.
6. Prick each cracker 2-3 times with a fork.
7. Bake for approximately 20 minutes until very lightly browned. Crackers around the edge may crisp up faster, so pull them out first if needed.
8. Cool for 5 min before breaking up the crackers
