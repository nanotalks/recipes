# 龙虾意面 - Lobster E-Fu Noodles

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] 1 disk of 意面 e-noodles
- [ ] 1.5 - 2lb lobster
- [ ] 2 stalks of green onion
- [ ] Ginger
- [ ] Cornstarch
- [ ] White pepper
- [ ] Granulated sugar
- [ ] Oyster sauce
- [ ] Sesame oil
- [ ] Canola oil
- [ ] Shaoxing cooking wine

## 2. Lobster Frying
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **1.5-2 lb** live lobster | **2 tbsp** of cooking wine |
| **3 tbsp** of cornstarch | Canola oil |

1. Preheat the oil in a pot to 350°F or the air fryer to 395°F.
2. Split the live lobster head width-wise along the groove, then the remaining lobster body and taillength wise.
3. Discard the gills under the head, trim the antennas, tips off the walking legs and the swimmerets under the tail.
4. Reserve the tomalley (the dark paste like material found inside the head) in a bowl with the cooking wine.
5. Cut the two halves of the lobster body and tail into 1-inch segments
6. Cut the large claws length-wise between the pincers
7. Cover the exposed lobster meat with light coating of cornstarch. The shell does not need cornstarch.
8. Deep fry the lobster segments for 10 seconds, or air fryer for 2 minutes one side, and another mintue on the other. 
9. *Optional:* Coat the lobster lightly with canola oil when using an air fryer.
10. Set aside on a wire rack to cool

## 3. Noodles
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **1 disk** of e-noodle | **2 tbsp** of canola oil |
| **4 large slices** of ginger | **1/2 tsp** of white pepper |
| **1/2 tsp** of granulated sugar | **4 tsp** of oyster sauce | 
| **1 tbsp** of sesame oil | **2 stalks** of green onion, chopped |

1. Bring a pot of water to boil
2. Add the noodles into the pot and let boil for 60s
3. Strain the noodles, and quench in cold water immediately.
4. In a clean frying pan, heat **2 tbsp** of oil on medium
5. Add the ginger and fry until fragrant
6. Add the lobster and the cooking wine with the lobster tomalley. Cover immediately for 2 minutes
7. Mix together the sugar, white pepper, green onions, sesame oil, and oyster sauce, and add to the lobster
8. Add the noodles in and stir to mix
8. Cover another minute before removing from heat and serving.
