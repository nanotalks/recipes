# Honey Garlic Sauce

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] 3 cloves of garlic
- [ ] Light soy sauce
- [ ] Sweet soy sauce
- [ ] Honey
- [ ] Sesame oil

## 2. Sauce mix
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **1 tbsp** of light soy sauce | **1 tbsp** of sweet soy sauce |
| **4 tbsp** of honey | **1 tsp** of sesame oil |
| **3 cloves** of garlic, minced ||

1. Mix all the ingredients together
