# BBQ Rub

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] Garlic powder
- [ ] Crushed red pepper
- [ ] Black peppercorn (ground)
- [ ] Szechuan peppercorn
- [ ] Smoked paprika powder
- [ ] Brown sugar
- [ ] Table salt

## 2. Rub mix
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|

| **1 tbsp** of smoked paprika powder | **2 tsp** of table salt |
| **1 tsp** of crushed red pepper | **1 tsp** of cinnamon|
