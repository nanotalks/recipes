# Vietnamese Pho Spice Pouch

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] 10 star anise
- [ ] 8 cloves
- [ ] 6 cinnamon sticks, 3-inch long
- [ ] 6 cardamom pods
- [ ] 2 tbsp coriander seeds
- [ ] 2 tbsp fennel seeds
- [ ] 2 tsp white peppercorn

## 2. Assembly
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **10** star anise, whole | **8** cloves, whole |
| **6** cinnamon sticks | **6** cardamom pods |
| **2 tbsp** of coriander seeds, whole | **2 tbsp** of fennel seeds, whole |
| **2 tsp** of white peppercorn, whole | |

1. Place ingredients into a reusable spice pouch, tying securely.
