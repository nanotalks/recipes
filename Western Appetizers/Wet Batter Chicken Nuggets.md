# Wet Batter Chicken Nuggets

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] **2lb** of chicken breast
- [ ] **1** large egg
- [ ] **2 cups** of all-purpose flour
- [ ] **3/4 cup** of milk
- [ ] **1-2L** of canola oil
- [ ] Paprika
- [ ] Garlic powder
- [ ] Onion powder
- [ ] Table salt
- [ ] Mustard

## 2. Marination
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **2lb** chicken breast | **1 tsp** paprika |
| **1 tsp** garlic powder | **1 tsp** onion powder |
| **1/2 tsp** salt | |

1. Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces, approximately 1-2 inches
2. In a large bowl, combine all the dry spices
3. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and toss until evenly coated
4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (can be done overnight)

## 3. Wet Batter & Frying
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **1-2L** of oil | **1** large eggs |
| **3/4 cup** milk | **2 tsp** of onion powder |
| **1 tbsp** of garlic powder | **2 tsp** of mustard |
| **1/2 tsp** of salt | **2 cup** all-purpose flour |

1. Heat oil for deep frying to 350°F (175°C) in a pot
2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, onion powder, garlic powder, mustard, salt and **1 cup** of flour until well combined
3. Place **1 cup** of flour in a separate shallow bowl
4. Remove the marinated chicken from the refrigerator
5. Working **5-8 piece** at a time, dip the chicken into the flour bowl first and cover completely
6. Dredge the chicken in the wet batter and shake off excess
7. Carefully place the battered chicken nuggets into the hot oil in batches, being careful not to overcrowd
8. Fry for 2 minutes before flipping the nuggets
9. Continue to fry for a minute, before removing and placing on a paper towel-lined plate or wire rack
10. Repeat with remaining chicken nuggets
11. Once the batch is complete, return the nuggets, 8 pieces at a time, back into the oil for 2 minutes, until golden brown and crispy
12. Allow the nuggets to cool on a paper towel-lined plate or wire rack

## 4. Freezing & Reheating

1. Lay the nuggets out in a tray, 1 layer thick, to cool
2. Arrange the cooled nuggets in a single layer on a parchment-lined baking sheet
3. Place the baking sheet in the freezer for at least 2 hours until the nuggets are solidly frozen
4. Transfer the frozen nuggets into freezer bags, removing as much air as possible
5. Label the bags with the date and store in the freezer for up to 3 months
6. To reheat, preheat your air fryer to 350°F (175°C)
7. Place frozen nuggets in the air fryer basket in a single layer without overlapping
8. Air fry for 6 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through cooking
9. The nuggets should be heated through and crispy; serve immediately
