# Corn on the Cob - Microwave

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] Corn on the cob
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Black pepper *(Optional)*

## 2. Microwave

|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **1** ear of corn, husk on | **1 tbsp** of butter |
| Salt | Black pepper *(Optional)* |

1. Place the ear of corn in the microwave with the husk still on — do not remove the husk or silk.
2. Microwave on high for 4 min for one ear. Add 2 minutes per additional ear
3. Remove from microwave using a kitchen towel.
4. Clean the husk and silk off the corn, top with butter, salt and pepper to taste, and serve immediately.
