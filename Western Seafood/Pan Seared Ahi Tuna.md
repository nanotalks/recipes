# Pan Seared Ahi Tuna

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] 2 ahi tuna steaks (about 113g - 170g each)
- [ ] 1 green onion
- [ ] 1 lime
- [ ] Canola oil
- [ ] Sesame oil
- [ ] Honey
- [ ] Toasted sesame seeds
- [ ] Black peppercorn
- [ ] Garlic powder
- [ ] Ginger powder
- [ ] Seafood soy sauce
- [ ] Wasabi *(Optional)*

## 2. Marinate
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **2 tbsp** of seafood soy sauce | **1 tbsp** of sesame oil |
| **1 tsp** of honey | **1 tsp** of ginger powder |
| **1 tsp** of garlic powder | **1/2 stalk** of green onion, minced |
| **3/4** lime, juiced | **2** ahi tuna steaks, patted dry |

1. Mix all the ingredients in a dish.
2. Place marinade tuna for 15 minutes for thinner cuts, flipping ever 5 minutes.

## 3. Pan Sear
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **1 tbsp** of canola oil | **1 tsp** of ground black peppercorn |

1. Heat canola oil in pan over medium-high heat.
2. Place the tuna steaks in the pan and sear for 1 min per side.
3. Torch the edges of the tuna if necessary, for thicker steaks.
4. Transfer the tuna to a cutting board and rest for 2 minutes before slicing.

## 4. Serve
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| **1/2** green onion, minced | **1 tsp** toasted sesame seeds |
| **1/2** lime, cut into wedges *(Optional)* | Wasabi *(Optional)* |

1. Slice the tuna across the grain into 1/4-inch thick pieces.
2. Sprinkle with green onion and toasted sesame seeds.
3. *(Optional)* Serve with lime wedges and wasabi.
