# Tofu Sheet Skewers - Air Fryer

## 1. Shopping List
- [ ] Tofu sheets *(not bean curd sheets)*
- [ ] Cumin powder
- [ ] Smoked paprika powder
- [ ] Hoisin sauce
- [ ] Canola oil
- [ ] Black peppercorn

## 2. Airfry
|<!-- -->|<!-- -->|
|---|---|
| Tofu sheets, cut into long strips | **1 tbsp** of canola oil |
| **1 tbsp** of hoisin sauce | **pinch** of cumin powder |
| **pinch** of smoked paprika powder | **pinch** of black pepper corn, ground |

1. Preheat the air fryer on max, to 510Â°F
2. Cut the tofu sheets into long, 4cm strips
3. Mix together the canola oil and hoisin sauce, and brush on one side of the tofu strips
4. Roll the strips up, and skewer the rolls to hold in place
5. Brush the remaining sauce on the outside of the rolls
6. Sprinkle cumin powder, paprika powder and black peppercorn over the rolls
7. Grill the skewers for 1 min, flip and grill for another minute before serving.
